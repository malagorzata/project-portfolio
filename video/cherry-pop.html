BASIC CONTENT

CHERRY POP PROJECT

Programs used: Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator

TASK

TASK

The objective of this project was to create three videos for start-up Cherry Pop. The videos were supposed to be be put on "pretotype" website and be aimed at teenagers, parents or teachers. The videos had to be made in social media format and presented in "dummy" version on the website. This project was made in a group. Therefore, this assignment included groupwork, working with basic film language, different video formats as well as research and presentation.

PROCESS

PROCESS

The process consisted of desk research and creating personas profiles basing on that. After gathering information, there was idea generation and creating storyboard with narrative curve. We decided on creating three different videos - all including animations. We wanted to include illustrations to portray pop-art style presented by the brand. This required utilising programs such as After Effects and Animation as well as Premiere Pro. The workload was divided into creating illustrations, website, putting videos together and adding finishing touches. I was in charge of the long video's animation what required animating them and synchronising with sounds and movement.

RESULT

RESULT

This assignment has improved my project management through utilising platforms such as Trello as well as keeping all the files on Teams. Additionally, I have learnt After Effects by myself to put our idea to life. Through using narrative curve, we could set the right time to build the momentum and introduce shocking element. This project has taught me the digital production flow and different forms of video as well as their usage on social media. Lastly, I gained understanding of Pecha Kucha presentation that forced me to be concise and to the point.
