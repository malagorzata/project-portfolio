BASIC UX

ONLINE T-SHIRT SHOP / UX DESIGN

Programs used: XD, Photoshop, Illustrator

TASK

TASK

This assignment aimed to teach us UX Design, its process and the groupwork. The main focus was around the user experience (UI and UX side of it) with the correct elements for the chosen target audience. Therefore, one of the key elements of the assignment was data collection and research to stand out amongst competition and engage the target user.

PROCESS

PROCESS

The process started with individual data collection that has allowed me to get insight to the t-shirt market. In the group, after collectively creating the paper prototype and storyboard, my role was to conduct one of Think Aloud tests. This has taught me the importance of the user insights. One of the main issues spotted was lack of clickability of buttons such as logo that user intuitively chose as 'return to homepage' button. Thus, this resulted in change of a design. Lastly, I was in charge of designing the home, contact and about us pages. The main point was to ensure the seamless customer flow as well as maintaing the same branding across all pages.

RESULT

RESULT

This group project has taught me the importance of small steps in the website flow that make big difference in the customer journey. Therefore, user testing is crucial part of the UX process. Additionially, I have learnt the creative process of creating UX/UI designs that includes sketches, idea generation, collaboration as well as storyboarding. Through fully understanding the consumers, their habits and the market, I was able to better adjust to their needs and potential obstacles in the flow.

Part of UX Design for PONAMAC's online t-shirt shop
