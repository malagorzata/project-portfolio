SILFEN STUDIO WEBSITE REDESIGN

Programs/languages used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, XD, Github, Restdb.io

TASK

TASK

For this assignment we worked with a real brand called Silfen Studio. The client wanted to improve their online sales through improving their website as they didn’t see enough conversion. Additionially, they wanted a solution for implementing their new sub-brand called Silfen Play.

PROCESS

PROCESS

The process started with conducting survey and interviews regarding Silfen’s website and online shopping in general. This has allowed us to understand potential problems with the design and the shopping flow. Later, we created a prototype in XD basing on the current design trends and users’ preferences. This was tested with Think Aloud Test. To implement Silfen Play, we added a ‘switch’ to enable users to go to a different brand. TBC
